Detailed Description

Represents a camera from where the game scene is seen.

A trackball camera always focuses on a target which has a certain position in the scene.

staticconstexprprivate

Angle factor for position offset.

Angle factor is the trigonometric tangent of lateral position and effective distance. The higher the factor is, the larger the position offset can be.
